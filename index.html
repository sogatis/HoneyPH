Our Mission



Honey Cats Ph is a small rescue group based in Taguig that promotes Spaying and Neutering stray cats in order to help control their population. We also do stray feeding, rescuing, and we also help out indigent pet owners when needed. Our group is continously powered by our love for animals, whom often times needs help but can't speak, with that said, we are here to speak up for them with the continous help and support by the online community. We highly promote adoption after rehabilitation so that the street angels can get the love and family that they deserve.



Our Goal



38 rescued cats and 74 cats fixed as of today, we want to continue and help more pets in need. We plan to continously trap and release community cats and continue feeding them, and also eventually finding them homes. We aim to have more fundraisers to help us continue with our projects.
